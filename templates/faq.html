Frequently Asked Questions

Your most common questions, answered simply.

How do I book a property?

Create an account, browse properties, and click “Book Now.”

How do payments work?

All payments are handled securely through our payment provider.

What if a host cancels?

You’ll get a full refund and assistance in finding a new place.

How do I list my property?

Open your profile and select “Add Listing.”

How do I contact support?

Use the Contact page to send us a direct message.
